Apaleo

Positioning Customer Research Category Strategy Enterprise Sales

Reframing category expectations in hospitality tech

Apaleo entered a market dominated by feature-rich, monolithic property management systems. Its narrative didn’t match how buyers evaluated solutions. Customer research showed buyers valued the API-first model, but hesitated due to perceived feature gaps and the operational overhead of a broader tech stack.

  • Ran structured customer research to identify adoption friction and expectation gaps
  • Redefined ICP segmentation and competitive framing
  • Built positioning + messaging hierarchy anchored in long-term flexibility and time-to-value
  • Developed differentiated product stories used across sales and industry events (“Hospitality, your way.”)
Impact Improved pipeline quality and sales confidence; contributed to six-figure enterprise deal wins; strengthened expectation management and faster time-to-value.

Adform — DCO

Repositioning AI/ML Optimization Messaging Systems Sales Enablement

Repositioning performance-driven personalization in the AI era

As generative AI reshaped market expectations, “personalization” increasingly implied automated creative generation. DCO’s core strength was different: modular creative orchestration and real-time performance optimization across video and HTML formats. The positioning had to evolve without overselling capability.

  • Analyzed shifting buyer perception and narrative gaps versus market context
  • Reframed DCO around asset leverage, orchestration, and measurable performance
  • Clarified modular assembly logic (dynamic selection of video/image/shell components)
  • Built 50+ structured use cases and activation “recipes” with Solutions + Implementation teams
  • Delivered enablement aligned to outcomes (efficiency, ROI), not AI hype
Impact Realigned the narrative to market maturity; enabled ROI-led sales conversations; improved internal clarity across commercial teams.
