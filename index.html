Adform — DCO

Repositioning a technically complex adtech system in a shifting AI landscape

Repositioning Technical Product Marketing Narrative Architecture Enterprise Enablement

Adform’s Dynamic Creative Optimization (DCO) platform operates within programmatic advertising, enabling brands to personalize and optimize digital creative at scale.

As generative AI shifted market expectations, “personalization” increasingly implied automated creative production. DCO’s strength was different: a modular system that assembles creative components and applies ML-driven decisioning to select the highest-performing variant at impression level across video and HTML formats.

  • Repositioned the product around system orchestration, asset leverage, and measurable performance
  • Translated modular assembly logic and optimization constraints into commercially clear narratives
  • Partnered with Product and Engineering to ensure positioning reflected real technical capabilities
  • Built 50+ structured use cases and activation “recipes” with global customer-facing teams
  • Delivered enablement frameworks anchored in ROI, asset efficiency, and deployment speed
Impact Elevated technical credibility in enterprise sales conversations and reduced expectation mismatch across global accounts.
